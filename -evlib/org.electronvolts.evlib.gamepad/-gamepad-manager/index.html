This class wraps a gamepad and adds: Edge detection to the digital inputs (buttons and dpad) {@see DigitalInput} Scaling to the analog inputs (joysticks and triggers) {@see AnalogInput}

Specify nothing for scalingFunction to opt out of joystick scaling
